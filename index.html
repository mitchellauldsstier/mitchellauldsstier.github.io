Born in 1989 in Redkey, Indiana, I was raised to ask questions and explore my interests. From a young age, I exhibited my precocious side – reading voraciously, disassembling electronics, and pursuing quantitative challenges. During high school, I became captivated by the financial markets after learning about their complexity – markets are an enigma that devoted professionals fail to conquer. There is no truer an illustration of this than the Great Financial Crisis.

Currently, I focus on investing in private, middle-market companies in a variety of sectors. As a private equity professional, I have substantial involvement in transaction evaluation, execution, portfolio monitoring and harvesting of control and minority stakes in distressed, stable, and growing companies. My industry experience includes niche manufacturing, distribution, business services, consumer retail, as well as healthcare and education. In addition to my career in finance, I work with several non-profit organizations that engage in community development and poverty alleviation.

E D U C A T I O N
Mitchell graduated from the Kelley School of Business of Indiana University in 2012 earning a Bachelor of Science degree in Business while majoring in both Finance and Management. He was ranked third in his class of 500 students, earning Highest Distinction.

During his undergraduate studies, Mitchell immersed himself in finance courses and a number of extracurricular and leadership activities. He was part of the Finance Development Enterprise Program (FDEP, a program typically designated for MBA students and organized by the Chair of the Evening MBA Program) which then led to an internship at a local, buy-side mergers and acquisitions practice. Additionally, in December of 2012, he was sponsored by the Dean of Research at the Kelley School to sit for the Chartered Financial Analyst Level I Exam.

In the coming months, Mitchell plans to apply to graduate school to earn his Masters in Business Administration. He is currently working through the application process and visiting a variety of campuses including Columbia, Yale, Harvard, Wharton, and the University of Chicago. He has taken the GMAT and is excited to see where these new experiences will lead.


C A R E E R
Mitchell began his career in investment banking with BB&T Capital Markets, a southeastern, regional investment bank headquartered in Richmond, Virginia. As an associate in the bank’s Leadership Development Program (LDP), he was designated as a Capital Markets Associate focusing on the areas of investment banking, equity research, and sales and trading.

Ultimately, he was selected to join the debt capital markets team within investment banking. He spent the next several years working with industry-leading professionals to raise capital from institutional investors for projects in the education, healthcare, and affordable housing sectors. Following this, Mitchell transitioned to the associate role with Pittsburgh’s largest private equity investment fund. Currently, Mitchell focuses on investing in private, middle-market companies in a variety of sectors.
